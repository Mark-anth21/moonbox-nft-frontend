boxes category

-users pay more to have more chances owning rare nfts. wood boxes: total 500 boxes require 0.5B moonshot to open, 2% chance to get top tier nfts. silver boxes: total 300 boxes, require 1B moonshot to open, 5% chance to get top tier nfts. golden boxes: total 150 boxes, require 2B moonshot to open, 10% chance to get top tier nfts. diamond boxes: total 50 boxes, require 10b moonshot to open, 20% chance to get top tier nfts.

Prize pool

-When users open boxes, the Moonshot tokens flow: 50% to marketing/development budget (pay for artist, dev to create even more NFTs), 50% to loot box prize pool. -Rare NFTs can be used to redeem MOONshot locked in prize pool: Tier god NFT: 10% total pool, 1 nft. tier 1 nft: 5% total pool, total 5 nfts. tier 2 nft: 0.5% total pool, total 15 nfts. tier 3 nft: 0.257% total pool, total 29 nfts. -maturity time: nfts that are opened need to wait to a certain time(10/20/30 days) to redeem moonshot. during that time they can trade their nft in free market (such as opensea).
